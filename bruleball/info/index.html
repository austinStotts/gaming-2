brule.ball
about
brule ball is a online, web based, multiplayer pvp game developed by me, steve
from the beggining i borrowed heavily from the rock-paper-scissors philosphy
in brule ball, the rock-paper-scissors is as follows...
throwing a ball is is beaten by a counter - a counter is beaten by a dash - and a dash is beaten by a throw
in detail, you can counter a thrown ball to negate all damage and send that ball back
then to avaid the countered ball you can dash away
but be careful! a thrown ball could be waiting for you at the end of that dash
the rock paper scissors is complemented with a cooldown based system
where all abilities are clearly shown to allow for counterplay

Abilities
throw
[left mouse]
throw 1 standard projectile
can be countered
  • cooldown: 1.5s
  • damage: 1
  • velocity: 100m/s
  • size: 0.5m
  • counter level:1
dash
[right mouse]
greatly increase horizontal velocity in the direction you are moving
not affected by where you are looking
  • cooldown: 1s
  • velocity burst:50m/s
counter
[E]
counter all damage for a short window
if timed well - a perfect counter will happen
perfect counters do a few extra things in addition to blocking/refecting projectiles
projectiles effected will have thier counter level raised by 1 and velocity increased
parry level determines the damage - all standard balls start at 1
a perfect counter will also not allow the ball to be countered by anything but another perfect counter
when you successfully perfect counter a ball the normal 1s cooldown will be instantly reset
perfect counters also give 1 charge of super
  • cooldown: 1s
  • counter window: 300ms
  • perfect counter window: 100ms
  • PC velocity bonus: (CLx20)m/s
super
[Q]
use super charge to launch a powerful projectile
projectile starts at counter level 2
can still be perfect countered
  • cost: 4
  • velocity: 200m/s
  • size: 1m
  • counter level: 2
